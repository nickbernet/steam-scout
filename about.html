Why I Built STEAMScout

Frustration Sparked the Idea

While students were getting recruited for sports, with websites, highlight videos, and scouts, I was spending late nights on robotics builds, programming sensors, and troubleshooting 3D printers. I had real skills, but nowhere to showcase them.

STEAM Talent Deserves Visibility

My name is Nick Bernet. I created STEAMScout because students building real-world solutions, whether in robotics, engineering or design deserve the same access to exposure and opportunity that athletes get.

Not Just a Resume – A Platform

STEAMScout lets students show off their projects, certifications, and competition history in a real, visual format. It connects them with recruiters, college programs, and companies that value innovation and initiative.

The Vision Going Forward

This isn’t just a tool, it’s a movement. STEAMScout is about giving students the power to turn their talent into traction. If you’re serious about building your future through STEM or the arts, this is where you start.
