How It Works

Step 1: Choose Your Role

Select whether you’re a student, a recruiter, or a company. This helps us customize your experience and give you access to the right tools.

Step 2: Build Your Profile

Students can add GPA, certifications, skill tags, competition history, project links, and more. Think of this as your digital portfolio, built to impress.

Step 3: Discover Opportunities

Recruiters and companies can search by skills, tags, and location. Students receive recommended programs, internships, and other matches based on their profile.

Step 4: Get Recruited

You’ve put in the work, and now it pays off. Whether it’s a summer internship, mentorship, or showcase invite, STEAMScout helps you connect with your next step.
